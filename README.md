# CPI_Index_Analysis_Excel
This project analyzes the All India Consumer Price Index (CPI) dataset containing monthly index values for multiple sectors across India.
✅ Q1 – Yearly CPI Trend Analysis

Objective: Identify overall inflation patterns across years.

Key Steps

Extracted Year and General Index columns.

Calculated year-on-year change in the CPI.

Computed average CPI per sector per year.

Insights

Clear upward trend in the General Index indicating sustained inflation.

Sectors like Fuel & Light and Housing showed stronger year-to-year spikes.

Essential commodities (Cereals, Vegetables) showed high volatility.

✅ Q2 – Month-wise Seasonal Variation

Objective: Analyze monthly seasonality patterns.

Key Steps

Grouped data by Month.

Computed average CPI for each sector across all years.

Identified seasonal highs and lows.

Insights

Vegetables and Fruits show predictable seasonal dips and spikes.

Fuel & Light remains relatively stable month-to-month.

Many essential food categories show price peaks in Q3 and Q4 months.

✅ Q3 – Sectoral Contribution to CPI

(Sheet: Q3.1)

Objective: Determine which sectors contribute most to CPI rise.

Key Steps

Calculated percentage contribution of each sector.

Compared sector weights with their inflation growth.

Ranked sectors by impact.

Insights

Food & Beverages categories contribute the largest share to CPI.

Housing has the highest weight, hence major impact on overall CPI.

Transport & Communication showed sharp increases due to fuel cost fluctuations.

✅ Q4 – Identifying High-Inflation Sectors

Objective: Identify the top sectors driving overall inflation.

Key Steps

Computed yearly inflation rate for each sector.

Detected outlier inflation spikes.

Ranked top 5 inflation-prone categories per year.

Key Findings

Oils & Fats, Vegetables, and Fuel & Light showed recurring high inflation.

Clothing & Footwear displayed steady long-term inflation without major fluctuations.

Education and Health showed gradual but persistent rise.

✅ Q5 – Time-Series Visualization & Insights

Objective: Visualize trends and interpret long-term movement.

Key Steps

Created time-series charts for General Index and sector indices.

Generated histograms for value distributions.

Identified long-term growth patterns.

Insights

Overall CPI increased significantly over the years.

Clear cyclical patterns in perishables (vegetables, fruits).

Index volatility highest in food sectors, lowest in housing and education.
